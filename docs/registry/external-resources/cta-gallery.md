## What it offers

A curated gallery of Call-to-Action (CTA) examples designed to inspire creativity and maximize conversions. Browsable by industry, interaction mode (button, form, modal, newsletter, pricing), and style. Includes a CTA-writing-tips guide and free Framer templates.

## When to consult

- WIZARD Stage 13 (Prototype Lab) — searching for CTA patterns that fit the project's persona.
- Conversion optimization sprints — borrowing patterns proven elsewhere.
- Copywriting + design pairing for landing pages.

## When NOT to consult

- B2B internal-tool projects with no public CTA surface.
- Already-locked design system that doesn't allow external CTA exploration.

## How to use

Visit the site and browse. Optionally subscribe to the newsletter for new examples.

## Fit signals

- Project has a public landing page or signup flow.
- Conversion rate is a tracked metric.
- Team needs to A/B test CTAs.

## Conflicts and overlaps

- Pairs with `ui-ux-pro-max` (UI exploration) and `copywriter-agent` (CTA copywriting).
