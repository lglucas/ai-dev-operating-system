## What it is

Curated learning resource for becoming a data engineer. Aggregates bootcamps, books, communities, projects, interview guides, and lists of tools, companies, and creators across data engineering. 25+ books, 10+ communities, 20+ newsletters, plus interviews guidance.

## When to consult

- Data-engineer hires onboarding.
- Project requires data pipelines, warehousing, or orchestration and team needs ramp-up.
- Choosing a data-engineering stack (Spark vs Beam, dbt vs custom, etc).

## When NOT to consult

- Project doesn't involve data engineering.
- Already proficient data-engineering team.
